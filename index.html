The craft

Nothing here comes off a factory line

Every notebook starts as raw paper and thread, cut, folded, and stitched by hand in small batches. No two runs are exactly alike — that's the point.

  • Hand-stitched binding, not machine-stapled
  • Small batches — once a run sells out, it's gone

FAQ

Good to know

How long does delivery take?

Once your order is confirmed, local Dubai delivery typically takes 2-4 days depending on stock availability.

Can I customize a notebook?

Yes — message us with what you have in mind and we'll let you know what's possible for your batch.

Do you take bulk or corporate orders?

Yes, reach out via WhatsApp with your quantity and timeline and we'll work out a plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

Details are shared once your order is confirmed — cash on delivery and bank transfer are both supported.
