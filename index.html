FAQ

Good to know

How long does delivery take?

Once your order is confirmed, local Dubai delivery typically takes 2-4 days depending on stock availability. We also provide express delivery in Dubai

Can I customize a notebook?

Yes — message us with what you have in mind and we'll let you know what's possible for your batch.

Do you take bulk or corporate orders?

Yes, reach out via WhatsApp with your quantity and timeline and we'll work out a plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

Details are shared once your order is confirmed — cash on delivery , Card Payments and bank transfer are both supported.
