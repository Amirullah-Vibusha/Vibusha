Our Mission

A platform for artists the world overlooks

"Each brush stroke carries a story that deserves to be seen."

At Vibusha, we give a stand to artists who truly deserve appreciation but face constraints in reaching the world. We work closely with tribes across India — and beyond — whose art and heritage are endangered or on the verge of being forgotten, giving them a platform to showcase their culture in whatever form speaks to them.

We don't burden our artisans with quantity. Each artist decides how much they create and at what pace — no mass production, no two pieces identical. Every order carries a fixed share back to the artisan behind it, supporting and uplifting their craft directly.

  • Supporting artisans from endangered and lesser-known tribes, in India and around the world
  • No mass production — every artist sets their own pace and quantity
  • A fixed portion of every order goes directly back to the artisan
  • Every piece is one of a kind, shaped by the artist's own hands and thoughts

The Craft

Crafted by hand, loved by you

Stationery

Every notebook is hand-painted, hand-stitched, and shaped around how you actually think — lined for the focused, unlined for the free-flowing, dated or undated, entirely your call.

  • Pages blended from cotton, silk, and moss
  • Hand-painted covers — no two alike
  • Customized to how you write, sketch, and think
  • A portion of every order supports the artisan behind it

Resin

Built to preserve — a pressed flower, a pet's paw print, a memory you don't want to fade. Each piece is hand-poured and cured slowly, layer by layer.

  • Hand-poured and cured, layer by layer
  • Preserves keepsakes, fur, paw prints, or memories that matter
  • Every piece supports our stray animal care initiative
  • No two pours are ever identical

FAQ

Good to know

How long does delivery take?

Once your order is confirmed, local Dubai delivery typically takes 2-4 days depending on stock availability. We also provide express delivery in Dubai.

Can I customize a notebook or pen?

Yes — message us on Instagram with your order details and we'll let you know what's possible for your batch and the artisan working on it.

Do you take bulk or corporate orders?

Yes, reach out via WhatsApp with your quantity, customization requests if any, and timeline, and we'll work out a plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

Details are shared once your order is confirmed — cash on delivery, card payments, and bank transfer are all supported.

How does Vibusha support artisans and stray animals?

A fixed portion of every stationery order goes directly back to the artisan who made it. Every resin order contributes to our stray animal care initiative — feeding, vaccination, and care for the wounded.
